My Interest in Information Technology

An Ever Growing Interest in Time

I cannot define a single moment in time where my interest in IT was ignited.

Rather, my interest grew over the years after seeing new gadgets coming onto the market nearly as quickly as a previous model sold out. And with every new model brought to market, was new innovative technologies that would change the world.

It seems there is no limit to the imagination and what people can achieve with the right training.

I personally have no experience in IT related work, however I believe that given the right training, I too, can achieve wonderful things in this world.
